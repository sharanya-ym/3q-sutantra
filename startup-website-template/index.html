Certifications

3Q launched an exclusive Advanced Professional Certification platform first time in India for fresh graduates in the field of Infrastructure Management services. The graduates who feel they have acquired enough skills can directly proceed to 3Q Certification exams and get themselves as Certified professionals which is a game changer in attracting high demand/paid corporate jobs by adding the 3Q official badges on their CVs. Those who wish to gain knowledge can undergo training/internships offered at 3Q or 3Q authorized training partners across the country.

Our Certifications

3Q Certified

Network Security Specialist
The Network Security Specialist certification focuses on developing skills to protect networks from cyber threats, ensuring the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of data. This program is designed for those pursuing a career in network defense and security management.

3Q Certified

Data Center Specialist
Our Data Center Specialist certification program provides the knowledge and hands-on experience required to design, manage, and optimize data center operations. Ideal for professionals aiming to become experts in managing critical IT infrastructure.

3Q Certified

NOC Engineer
The NOC Engineer certification prepares individuals to monitor and maintain network infrastructure, ensuring network stability and uptime. This program is ideal for those aiming to excel in network operations and support roles.

3Q Certified

Network Expert
Our Network Expert certification program equips professionals with the advanced skills needed to design, manage, and troubleshoot complex networks, ensuring seamless connectivity and performance. This program is perfect for those looking to deepen their expertise in networking technologies.

3Q Certified

Network Professional
The Network Professional certification focuses on developing comprehensive skills in network design, deployment, and troubleshooting. This program is ideal for professionals looking to enhance their expertise and advance their careers in the networking domain.

3Q Certified

End User Support Engineer
The End User Support Engineer certification equips professionals with the knowledge to provide technical support, resolve end-user issues, and maintain IT systems. Ideal for those looking to pursue a career in IT support and troubleshooting.

3Q Certified

Server and Cloud Specialist
Our Server and Cloud Specialist certification program covers the design, deployment, and management of server and cloud-based infrastructures. This program is designed for professionals looking to enhance their skills in cloud technologies and server management.
